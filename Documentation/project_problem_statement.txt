Problem 1: Collaborative Weekly Report Management System
Theme: Smart Productivity Systems
Problem Statement
Design a web-based collaborative system to streamline the preparation of departmental
weekly reports at an educational institution.
Currently, weekly reports are prepared manually by collecting data from multiple faculty
members across 17 structured sections. This leads to delays, inconsistencies, missed
entries, and increased manual effort for coordinators. The system should enable structured
section-wise data entry, centralized management, multi-user collaboration, and automated
report generation in the institution's prescribed format.
